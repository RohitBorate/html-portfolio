About Me

As an Analyst Ethical Hacker at CyberXchange, I conduct comprehensive Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) for various platforms, including web applications, Android systems, and network infrastructure. I leverage my expertise in VAPT methodologies and tools, such as Burp Suite and Nessus, to identify and report security vulnerabilities and provide effective solutions.

I also facilitate communication and knowledge sharing with IT professionals and management, aligning security measures with organizational goals. Additionally, I mentor and guide freshers in their journey to becoming proficient cyber security professionals, equipping them with essential skills and techniques.

My passion for cyber security stems from my educational background in Computer Engineering, which I completed from MIT World Peace University in June 2022. During the pandemic, I realized the importance of learning and mastering VAPT and obtained multiple certifications from IBM in Cybersecurity Tools, Processes, and Compliance.

I am driven by the challenge of securing digital ecosystems and mitigating potential risks, and I am always eager to learn and stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in the field. I possess excellent problem-solving skills, a meticulous attention to detail, and the ability to effectively communicate technical concepts to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
